About the Brewer

He doesn’t chirp much. But when he brews, he listens.

Robin — yes, like the bird — is the quiet mind and steady hand behind the beer. He brews with a touch of wonder, a pinch of science, and an obsession with detail. When he’s not brewing, he’s probably watching the stars or crushing you in a pub quiz.

This brewery was built with patience, playlists, and a deep love of haze.
